White color is the color of purity, cleanliness and neutrality. Doctors wear white coats, brides traditionally wear white gowns and a white picket fence surrounds a safe and happy home.

In the psychology of color, the white color of new beginnings, wiping from a clean slate, so to speak. This is a blank canvas, waiting to be written upon. While white does not stimulate feelings, it opens the way to creating something that the mind can conceive.

White contains an equal balance of all colors of the spectrum, which represent both the positive and negative aspects of all colors. Its main function is equality, which means justice, impartiality, neutrality and independence.
